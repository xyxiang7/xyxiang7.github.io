I am a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at Peking University, China. I am fortunate to have worked with Prof. Xin Jin at Peking University, and Prof. Minlan Yu at Harvard University.

My research is focused on networked systems. I aim to build efficient, reliable and programmable distributed software systems for emerging high-throughput and low-latency applications.

Aside from my research area, I am broadly interested in computer systems, from high-level design of programming languages to low-level computer architecture.
